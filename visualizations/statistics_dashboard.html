⚠️ Important Note on Population Estimates

  • Population figures based on ABS December 2024 Household Survey estimates (45,000+ respondents). These are estimates with confidence intervals, not exact counts.
  • Official 2026 Census data including sexual orientation and gender identity will be released in June 2027. At that time, all incident rates will be recalculated with official Census figures.
  • Current data allows for state-by-state comparison but should be considered preliminary pending Census release.
  • Source: Australian Bureau of Statistics - Estimates and Characteristics of LGBTI+ Populations in Australia (December 2024)
