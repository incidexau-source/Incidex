Methodology & Data Sources

LGBTIQ+ Voting Alignment Score represents the proportion of pro-LGBTIQ+ votes by each MP on tracked federal legislation.

Bills Tracked (2017-2025):

  • Marriage Amendment (Definition and Religious Freedoms) Act 2017
  • Religious Discrimination Bill 2021 (position on bill)
  • Anti-Discrimination Act amendments
  • Conversion practices ban proposals
  • Gender recognition / Trans rights legislation

Score Calculation: Base party alignment adjusted by individual voting record. Score ranges: Strong Supporter (85-100%), Supporter (70-84%), Mixed (50-69%), Opponent (30-49%), Strong Opponent (0-29%).

Data Sources: Parliament of Australia (aph.gov.au), They Vote For You (theyvoteforyou.org.au), Australian Electoral Commission, Hansard Records.
