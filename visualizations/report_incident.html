Report an Incident

Your report helps document LGBTIQ+ hate crimes for advocacy and awareness. All submissions are anonymous and encrypted.

Important Information

You are not required to report to police. This portal is independent and community-led. Your report helps document hate crimes for advocacy purposes.

If you want police involvement, contact the Police Assistance Line: 131 444 or Emergency: 000.

Your submission will be fully encrypted and stored securely. Personal details are never shared without your explicit consent.
