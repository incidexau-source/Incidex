Frequently Asked Questions

How can I report an incident?
Select "Report Incident" above and provide as much detail as you're comfortable sharing. Anonymous reports are accepted.
Will my identity be protected?
Yes. Personal details are never published. You can also choose to submit completely anonymously.
How can I volunteer?
We're always looking for help with data verification, research, and outreach. Select "Volunteer" and tell us about your skills.
Can I use your data for research?
Yes! Our data is available for academic and advocacy purposes. Contact us to discuss your project.
