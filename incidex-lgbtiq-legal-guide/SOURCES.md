# Sources: LGBTIQ+ Discrimination & Vilification Legal Guide

**Last updated: 31 December 2025**

This document lists all sources used in creating the Incidex LGBTIQ+ Legal Guide, organized by tier as specified in the project requirements.

---

## TIER 1: Primary Legislation (Non-Negotiable Foundation)

### Commonwealth

**Sex Discrimination Act 1984 (Cth)**
- URL: https://www.legislation.gov.au/C2004A02868/latest/text
- Compilation: C2025C00XXX (as at 1 November 2025)
- Key sections: s 4 (definitions), s 5A (sexual orientation), s 5B (gender identity), s 5C (intersex status), s 37-38 (religious exemptions), Part II (unlawful discrimination)

**Fair Work Act 2009 (Cth)**
- URL: https://www.legislation.gov.au/C2009A00028/latest/text
- Key sections: s 351 (general protections), s 772 (discrimination)

### New South Wales

**Anti-Discrimination Act 1977 (NSW)**
- URL: https://legislation.nsw.gov.au/view/html/inforce/current/act-1977-048
- Current from: 1 July 2024
- Key sections: s 4 (definitions), s 38A (transgender), s 49ZG (homosexuality), s 49ZH (small employer exemption), s 56 (religious exemptions), Part 4C (vilification)

**Equality Legislation Amendment (LGBTIQA+) Act 2024 (NSW)**
- Passed: October 2024
- Commenced: 1 July 2025 (gender recognition provisions)
- Note: Updated birth certificate provisions but did NOT update Anti-Discrimination Act

**Crimes Act 1900 (NSW)**
- URL: https://legislation.nsw.gov.au/view/html/inforce/current/act-1900-040
- Key sections: s 93Z (inciting violence)

### Victoria

**Equal Opportunity Act 2010 (Vic)**
- URL: https://www.legislation.vic.gov.au/in-force/acts/equal-opportunity-act-2010
- Current from: 20 September 2025
- Key sections: s 4 (definitions - updated Sept 2025), s 6-8 (protected attributes), s 82-84 (religious exemptions - amended 2021), Part 6 (complaint process)

**Equal Opportunity (Religious Exceptions) Amendment Act 2021 (Vic)**
- Passed: 2021
- Commenced: Various dates
- Narrowed religious exemptions

**Racial and Religious Tolerance Act 2001 (Vic)**
- URL: https://www.legislation.vic.gov.au/in-force/acts/racial-and-religious-tolerance-act-2001
- Note: Vilification protections for race and religion only, NOT LGBTIQ+

### Queensland

**Anti-Discrimination Act 1991 (Qld)**
- URL: https://www.legislation.qld.gov.au/view/html/inforce/current/act-1991-085
- Reprint current from: 19 May 2025
- Key sections: Schedule (Dictionary - definitions updated 29 April 2024), s 7 (protected attributes), s 25 (religious exemptions), s 28 (work with children - REPEALED), Part 3A (vilification)

**Anti-Discrimination and Other Legislation Amendment Act 2024 (Qld)**
- Passed: Early 2024
- Commenced: 29 April 2024
- Added "sex characteristics", expanded "gender identity" definition

**Respect at Work and Other Matters Amendment Act 2024 (Qld)**
- Passed: September 2024
- Commencement: Majority of provisions commence 1 July 2025
- Note: NOT yet in force as at 31 Dec 2025

### Western Australia

**Equal Opportunity Act 1984 (WA)**
- URL: https://www.legislation.wa.gov.au/legislation/statutes.nsf/law_a767.html
- Key sections: s 4 (definitions), s 66ZD (dress and appearance), s 72-73 (religious exemptions), Part IXB (racial vilification only)

### South Australia

**Equal Opportunity Act 1984 (SA)**
- URL: https://www.legislation.sa.gov.au/lz?path=%2FC%2FA%2FEQUAL%20OPPORTUNITY%20ACT%201984
- Current from: 1 September 2023
- Key sections: s 5 (definitions), s 37 (education), s 50 (religious exemptions), s 66A (dress and appearance), Part 4A (racial vilification only)

### Tasmania

**Anti-Discrimination Act 1998 (Tas)**
- URL: https://www.legislation.tas.gov.au/view/html/inforce/current/act-1998-046
- Current from: 1 July 2025
- Key sections: s 3 (definitions - most inclusive in Australia), s 16 (protected attributes), s 52 (religious exemptions - strongest protections), Part 3 (prohibited conduct including vilification)

### Australian Capital Territory

**Discrimination Act 1991 (ACT)**
- URL: https://www.legislation.act.gov.au/a/1991-81
- Republication effective: 16 November 2025
- Key sections: Dictionary (definitions - "sex characteristics" added Aug 2020), s 7 (protected attributes), s 32-33 (religious exemptions - amended 2019 and 2023), Part 3 (vilification)

### Northern Territory

**Anti-Discrimination Act 1992 (NT)**
- URL: https://legislation.nt.gov.au/en/Legislation/ANTI-DISCRIMINATION-ACT-1992
- In force: 31 October 2025
- Key sections: s 4 (definitions - "sex characteristics" added late 2022), s 19 (protected attributes), s 20A (offensive behaviour - added late 2022, amended Oct 2025), s 37B (religious exemptions - amended 2023 and 2025), s 51 (religious exemptions)

**Anti-Discrimination Amendment Act 2022 (NT)**
- Commenced: 3 July 2023
- Added sex characteristics, vilification protections, positive duty

**Anti-Discrimination Amendment Act 2025 (NT)**
- Passed: October 2025
- Re-introduced narrower religious exemption, altered offensive behaviour provisions

### Law Reform Commission Reports

**WA Law Reform Commission**
- "Review of the Equal Opportunity Act 1984 (WA)" (August 2022)
- Recommended: add sex characteristics, update gender identity definition, narrow religious exemptions, add vilification protections
- Status: WA Government committed to reforms but not implemented as at 31 Dec 2025

**Queensland Human Rights Commission**
- "Review of Queensland's Anti-Discrimination Act 1991" (2022)
- Recommended: new Anti-Discrimination Act
- Status: Government committed to new Act before end of 2023 but not delivered as at 31 Dec 2025

**NSW Law Reform Commission**
- Review of Anti-Discrimination Act 1977 (ongoing as at 2025)
- Announced 2023, still ongoing as at 31 Dec 2025

**Australian Law Reform Commission**
- Religious Exemptions Inquiry (Commonwealth)
- Report due 31 December 2023, handed to Attorney-General but not yet tabled as at 31 Dec 2025

## Conflicts and Resolutions

2. **Tier 2 (official bodies) prevailed** over Tier 3 when interpreting legislation
3. **Tier 3 used for verification only**, never as primary authority
